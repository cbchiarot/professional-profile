Health Economist & Epidemiologist

Cameron B. Chiarot

Cameron B. Chiarot, a health economist and epidemiologist with over twenty years of executive leadership experience within Canada's largest health systems, is currently pursuing a senior scientist position where he can leverage his distinctive combination of strategic expertise and rigorous research. He is scheduled to defend his PhD in Public Health Sciences in November 2025, after engaging in extensive international collaboration and fieldwork in Low- and Middle-Income Countries. His doctoral research employed a broad spectrum of complex modelling techniques to assess maternal and child health, infectious diseases, and outpatient service utilization in response to climate-related shocks and chronic health system stressors. His principal strengths include translating intricate data into practical strategies and bridging the divide between scientific innovation and real-world application.
